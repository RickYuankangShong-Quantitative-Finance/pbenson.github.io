Invite to 2017 Princeton Quant Trading Conference

Dear Michigan Students and Alumni,

We are pleased to invite you to the 2017 IIT Stuart-Princeton Quant Trading Conference at IIT Stuart School of Business in Chicago, IL. As Michigan students, young alumni and faculty, you can use the discount code MICHIGAN50 for an additional 50% off the tickets registration price. In case you are interested in receiving a discount code for the Portfolio Effect or Quantopian Workshop tickets sign up with your email address here.

The conference will feature speakers and panelists from leading trading firms, banks, and hedge funds from around the country. Our speakers will provide real trading and quant insights from the trenches. There will be opportunities to network with leading professionals and fellow top students and gain an insider's view of this fast-paced and exciting sector of finance as well as learn about internship and employment opportunities.

Over the past six years, we’ve brought together more than 100 high-level industry professionals and 2,000 students in Princeton and in Chicago to discuss the leading trends in quantitative trading. This year’s fall conference will take place on Monday, October 23rd, at IIT Stuart School of Business in Chicago, IL. Confirmed speakers so far include:

  • Stephanie Toper, Director of Portfolio Analytics at PortfolioEffect
  • Brian Peterson, Director of Quantitative Trading at DV Trading
  • Yuhua Yu, Portfolio Manager at Dijun Capital
  • Peter Cotton, Executive Director at JP Morgan
  • Marco Santoli, Quantitative Trader at IMC Markets
  • Christina Qi, Partner at Domeyard LLP
  • Jeffrey Greco, Portfolio Manager at Milliman Financial Risk Management LLC
  • Petra Bakosova, Chief Operating Officer at Hull Tactical Funds
  • Delaney Mackenzie, Director of Academia at Quantopian
  • Robert Piton, Senior Portfolio Manager at Rothschild Investment Corporation
  • Jonathan Kinlay, Head of Research and Trading at Systematic Strategies
  • Bert Mouler, Principal at Profluent Capital

To qualify, students must use their umich.edu email for registration. To register, please visit our conference site or our Eventbrite page.
