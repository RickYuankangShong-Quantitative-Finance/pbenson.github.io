Getting Help

On Campus Group Study

Many students have found Group Study sessions helpful.

It is open to all students and is an opportunity to work with other students in a lab where a trained facilitator is there to answer questions. The trained facilitator is often a student who has taken and succeeded in the key computer classes at WCC. Group study is a way to build peer networks with fellow students at WCC.

It would be good to bring earbuds/headphones if you want to watch videos in a Group Study Session.

Discussion Boards

Each assignment will have a required class participation section associated with it. One way to participate for a given assignment is to post a question on one of the discussion boards. Another way to participate for a given assignment is to find a question to answer on the discussion board. Note that there are other class participation options, but the idea is to provide a social network for each class where we can have student-to-student interaction where you can help each other out.

The blackboard discussion board capability provides a capability where you can request an email sent to you when someone responds to your question.

It is not a good idea to post questions to the instructor on the discussion board. The discussion board is a good way to post a question to other students in the class.

GoToMeeting

If the discussion board and study group still isn't answering your question, send me an email to setup a GoToMeeting.
